Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between website and web app?

Websites are primarily informational with limited interactivity, while web applications are interactive platforms with user accounts, data processing, and complex functionality.

When will my project be deployed?

Our first deployment is scheduled for October 2025. New projects will be scheduled based on our development queue after that date.

Can I get a custom solution?

Websites are 100% custom. Web applications are built on our proven framework but customized to meet your specific business requirements.

What about ongoing maintenance?

The monthly fee includes ongoing maintenance, security updates, and minor improvements. Major feature additions may require separate development fees.
